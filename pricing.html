Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our plans? We've got answers

Can I change plans at any time?
▼
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate the charges fairly.
What happens if I cancel?
▼
You can cancel your subscription anytime with no penalty. Your access continues until the end of your current billing cycle. We'll never charge you again after cancellation.
Do you offer discounts for annual commitments?
▼
Yes! All our pricing is based on annual billing, which gives you a better rate than paying monthly. Contact our sales team to discuss quarterly or custom contract terms for Enterprise plans.
What payment methods do you accept?
▼
We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and Revolut Pay. Enterprise customers can arrange custom payment terms. Contact us for invoice-based billing options.
Is there a setup fee?
▼
No setup fees for Growth and Scale plans. You start paying your monthly subscription immediately. Enterprise plans include complimentary white-glove onboarding.
Can I use 9 Elms Labs for multiple websites?
▼
Growth and Scale plans are per-domain. Enterprise customers get access to multiple domains. Contact us if you need multi-domain setups on Growth or Scale plans—we may be able to work something out.
Do you offer refunds or credits?
▼
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not completely satisfied within 30 days of purchase, we'll refund 100% of your subscription fee. No questions asked.
What's included in the Scale plan's competitor tracking?
▼
Competitor tracking monitors up to 10 competitors' AI visibility scores, content changes, schema markup updates, and conversion optimization efforts. You get weekly alerts when competitors make significant changes.
