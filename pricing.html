Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our plans? We've got answers

What is the Free AI Audit?
▼
The Free AI Audit is a one-time scan that gives you an AI visibility score, SEO and conversion analysis, growth potential estimate with competitor comparison, and a PDF report emailed to you. No credit card required — it's completely free.
What's the difference between Starter, Growth, and Scale?
▼
Starter (£199/mo) provides monthly scans, reports, and 2 content recommendations — perfect for small businesses. Growth (£499/mo) upgrades to weekly scans and reports, adds 4 AI-written SEO articles with schema markup, competitor tracking, and alert emails. Scale (£999/mo) gives you daily monitoring, 8 articles, daily competitor intelligence, sensitive alerts, and quarterly full re-audits.
Can I change plans at any time?
▼
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate the charges fairly.
What happens if I cancel?
▼
You can cancel your subscription anytime with no penalty. Your access continues until the end of your current billing cycle. We'll never charge you again after cancellation.
What payment methods do you accept?
▼
We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and Revolut Pay. Contact us for invoice-based billing options.
Is there a setup fee?
▼
No setup fees on any plan. You start paying your monthly subscription immediately and can cancel anytime.
Can I use 9 Elms Labs for multiple websites?
▼
All plans are per-domain. Contact us if you need multi-domain setups — we can work out a custom arrangement.
Do you offer refunds or credits?
▼
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not completely satisfied within 30 days of purchase, we'll refund 100% of your subscription fee. No questions asked.
What AI-written articles are included in Growth and Scale?
▼
Growth includes 4 full AI-written SEO articles per month, and Scale includes 8. Every article comes with schema markup and is ready to publish on your website. They are optimised to improve your visibility across AI platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Copilot.
